About KAMEL PRO

KAMEL PRO is a modern, secure media player application designed to help you manage and organize your personal playlists.

What We Do

Media Player Only

KAMEL PRO is a media player. We do not provide, sell, or host any video content, TV channels, movies, or subscriptions.

Playlist Management

Easily add and organize your M3U and Xtream playlist links. All playlists are stored securely and synced to your activated device.

Multi-Device Activation

Activate your TV or Android device using MAC address and ID. Manage all your playlists from our web portal.

User Privacy First

We do not track what you watch. We do not store your playlist content. Your privacy and data security are our priority.

Important Notice

Users are solely responsible for the content they add to the application. KAMEL PRO does not provide any media content. All playlists must be legally obtained by the user.

Our Mission

To provide a simple, fast, and secure media player that respects user privacy and complies with all copyright laws.
