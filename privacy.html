Privacy Policy - KAMEL PRO

Last updated: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

KAMEL PRO is a media player application only. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect or store:

  • Any IPTV playlists, M3U links, or Xtream codes you enter
  • Any media content you play
  • Your viewing history or streaming activity

We only store:

  • MAC address and Activation ID you provide to link your device
  • Technical data needed for the app to function (device activation status, last seen)

3. How We Use Your Data

MAC address and Activation ID are used solely to activate your device and sync playlists you add. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your language preference and terms acceptance. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for secure database storage and Google AdSense for displaying ads. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase: https://supabase.com/privacy

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption. We implement security measures to protect your information.

7. User Responsibility

KAMEL PRO does not provide any media content, streams, or subscriptions. Users are solely responsible for the content they add and must ensure they have legal rights to access it. We do not monitor or control user-supplied content.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
